# Trading Guide

A comprehensive guide to candlestick patterns and trading methods.

## What's Covered

- Japanese Candlestick Charts
- Bullish and Bearish Reversal Patterns
- Continuation Patterns
- Doji Patterns
- Price Action Trading
- Order Flow Trading
- Algorithmic Trading
- High-Frequency Trading

## Topics

### Candlestick Charts
Learn how to read candlestick charts, understand the body and wicks, and interpret what they tell you about market movements.

### Pattern Recognition
Covers major patterns including Hammer, Inverted Hammer, Bullish Engulfing, Piercing Line, Morning Star, Three White Soldiers, Hanging Man, Shooting Star, Bearish Engulfing, Dark Cloud Cover, Evening Star, and Three Black Crows.

### Doji Patterns
Detailed breakdown of Standard Doji, Long-Legged Doji, Dragonfly Doji, and Gravestone Doji patterns.

### Price Action Trading
Pure technical analysis focused on reading raw price movements without indicators. Covers trend structure, support/resistance, pin bars, and inside bars.

### Order Flow Trading
Real-time analysis of buy and sell orders using footprint charts. Learn about delta, point of control, and volume imbalances.

### Algorithmic Trading
Overview of automated trading systems, including market making, arbitrage, trend following, mean reversion, and statistical arbitrage strategies.

### High-Frequency Trading
Understanding HFT strategies, co-location, latency arbitrage, and how institutional traders operate at microsecond speeds.
